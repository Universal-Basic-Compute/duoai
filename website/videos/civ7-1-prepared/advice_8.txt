Choose Greece for this game, as their unique abilities perfectly complement Benjamin Franklin's scientific and diplomatic traits. The Demokratia ability provides +3 influence per turn and +30% production towards the Oracle, which aligns with Franklin's production bonuses. Focus on building the Acropolis unique quarter to gain culture and happiness, and prioritize training Logios in cities to boost your scientific potential. Your strategy should revolve around leveraging Greece's cultural and diplomatic strengths while using Franklin's scientific bonuses to accelerate your technological development.
