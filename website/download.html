Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about downloading and installing DUOAI

Is DUOAI free to download?

Yes, DUOAI is free to download. We offer a free tier with limited features and premium subscription plans for enhanced capabilities. See our pricing page for details.

Will DUOAI work with all my games?

DUOAI is designed to work with most popular PC games. Our AI can provide general assistance for any game, but specialized features are available for our growing library of fully supported titles.

Is DUOAI safe to use with online games?

Yes, DUOAI is designed to be compliant with game terms of service. It provides advice and analysis but doesn't automate gameplay or modify game files. It uses screen capture technology that doesn't interfere with game integrity.

How do I update DUOAI?

DUOAI will automatically check for updates when launched. You can also manually check for updates in the settings menu. When an update is available, you'll be prompted to install it.

Can I use DUOAI on multiple computers?

Yes, you can install DUOAI on multiple computers and use it with the same account. However, our usage limits apply to your account across all devices.

How do I uninstall DUOAI?

You can uninstall DUOAI like any other application through your operating system's control panel or settings. Your account data will be preserved for when you reinstall.
