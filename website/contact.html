Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent technical support, please specify in your message subject.

I'm having technical issues with DUOAI. What should I do?

For technical support, please email support@duoai.com with details about your issue, including your operating system, game, and a description of the problem. Screenshots are helpful when applicable.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with DUOAI. Please contact our support team to process your refund.

How can I request a feature?

We love hearing feature suggestions! Please use the contact form with the subject "Product Feedback" or join our Discord community where we have a dedicated channel for feature requests.
