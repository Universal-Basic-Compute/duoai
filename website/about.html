Our Story

DUOAI was born from a vision to revolutionize how we interact with technology. As an experienced technologist with a background in AI and Web3, I saw an opportunity to create an intelligent assistant that truly understands the context of what you're doing.

Founded by Nicolas Lester Reynolds, DUOAI represents the culmination of years of experience in artificial intelligence, software development, and product design. With previous successful projects in the technology space, I set out to build the digital assistant I always wished existed - one that could provide intelligent, contextual help exactly when needed.

What started as a concept for gaming assistance has evolved into a broader vision for Universal Basic Compute ($UBC) - making advanced AI assistance accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Our Mission

I believe that advanced computing assistance should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide Universal Basic Compute ($UBC) - intelligent AI assistance that adapts to each user's unique needs and context.

We're committed to:

  • Making technology more accessible to users of all skill levels
  • Providing contextual assistance that understands what you're doing
  • Creating AI that respects user privacy and autonomy
  • Developing technology that enhances human capabilities rather than replacing them

Starting with gaming assistance, DUOAI is the first step toward a broader vision where everyone has access to powerful AI tools that make technology more intuitive and helpful.

Our Values

The principles that guide our work

Universal Access

We believe advanced AI assistance should be available to everyone, regardless of technical expertise or financial resources - the core principle of Universal Basic Compute ($UBC).

Integrity

We respect user privacy and autonomy. DUOAI is designed to assist and enhance human capabilities, not to exploit or replace them.

Innovation

We're constantly exploring new ways to apply AI technology to real-world challenges, pushing the boundaries of what's possible with contextual assistance.

Community

We believe in the power of community feedback and actively incorporate user insights into our development process to create technology that truly serves people's needs.
