About Us

The team behind DUOAI

Our Story

DUOAI was born from a simple frustration: getting stuck in games with no one to ask for help. As passionate gamers ourselves, we've all experienced those moments where we're facing a challenging boss, trying to solve a complex puzzle, or simply wondering if there's a better strategy.

Founded in 2022, our small team of three gaming enthusiasts set out to create the gaming companion we always wished we had. We combined our expertise in artificial intelligence, game development, and user experience design to build DUOAI - an intelligent assistant that understands games and gamers.

What started as a weekend project quickly evolved into our passion. After months of development and testing with fellow gamers, we're proud to bring DUOAI to the gaming community.

Our Mission

We believe that gaming should be accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone. Our mission is to enhance the gaming experience by providing intelligent assistance that adapts to each player's unique style and needs.

We're committed to:

  • Making games more accessible to players of all skill levels
  • Helping gamers improve their skills through personalized guidance
  • Creating technology that understands the nuances of different games
  • Respecting player privacy and game integrity

As gamers ourselves, we're building the tool we want to use - one that enhances the gaming experience without taking away the joy of discovery and achievement.

Our Values

The principles that guide our work

Gamers First

We build for fellow gamers, prioritizing features that enhance the gaming experience without compromising the challenge and satisfaction of gameplay.

Integrity

We respect the rules of games and the vision of game developers. DUOAI is designed to assist, not to exploit or automate gameplay.

Innovation

We're constantly exploring new ways to apply AI technology to gaming challenges, pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Community

We believe in the power of the gaming community and actively incorporate player feedback into our development process.
